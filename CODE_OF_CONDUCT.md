# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want participation in this project — through issues, pull requests, or
discussion — to be a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background, experience level, nationality, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in feedback, especially in code reviews.
- Assume good faith; ask clarifying questions instead of jumping to
  conclusions about someone's intent or competence.
- Accept disagreement gracefully — technical decisions here are made by
  the maintainer, but reasoning will always be explained.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information (this applies doubly given this
  project touches real client business data — see `SECURITY.md`).
- Sustained disruption of issues, PRs, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private security advisory or
contacting [@Ziad-Thabet](https://github.com/Ziad-Thabet) directly on
GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and addressed at the maintainer's
discretion, which may include warning, temporary restriction, or a
permanent ban from the repository.

This document is adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant
(contributor-covenant.org), rewritten for this project rather than
reproduced verbatim.
